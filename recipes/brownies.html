Brownies

Main Page

Craving a fudgy, chewy, and chocalaty dessert? Everyone does at some point! This is just the recipe to satiate that desire!

Prep Time: 15-20 min.

Total Time: 45 min.

Makes: 16 servings

What You Need:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (optional)

How to Make it:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350F and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.

  2. Whisk together the melted butter and sugar until smooth, in a large mixing bowl.

  3. Add the eggs, on at a time, mixing well after each addition.

  4. Sift in cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder, and stir until well combined.

  5. Add the vanilla extract and flour, stirring until just combined.

  6. Fold in chocolate chips. (optional)

  7. Pour batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth out the top with a spatula.

  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the brownies comes out clean.

  9. Cool the brownies in the pan for at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy!
