Lemon Squares

Main Page

Do you ever crave a sour treat but don't want to drink boring lemonade? Try out these lemon squares! These tart treats are perfect for and easy treat!

Prep Time: 15 min.

Total Time: 50 min.

Makes: 24 Servings

What You Need:

Shortbread Base

  • 2 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Lemon Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 lemons, juiced

How to Make it:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.

  2. Mix 2 cups flour, 1 cup softened butter, and 1/2 cup sugar in a medium bowl until well combinded.

  3. Press into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13-inch pan.

  4. Bake until firm and goldin, about 15 minutes.

  5. Whisk remaining 1 1/2 cups sugar and 1/4 flour in medium bowl. Whisk in eggs, then lemon juice until smooth

  6. Bake for 20 minutes. Then set pan side to cool completely; the bars will firm up as the cool. When cooled, cut into uniform squares.

Enjoy!
