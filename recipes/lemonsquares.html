Lemon Squares

Main Page

Do you ever crave a sour treat but don't want to drink boring lemonade? Try out these lemon squares! These tart treats are perfect for and easy treat!

Prep Time: 15 min.

Total Time: 50 min.

Makes: 24 Servings

What You Need:

Shortbread Base

  • 2 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Lemon Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 lemons, juiced
